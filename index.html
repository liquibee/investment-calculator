Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the projections?

+

Our calculator uses the information you provide to make projections. While we strive for accuracy, actual results may vary due to market fluctuations and other factors.

Can I save my calculations?

+

Currently, we don't offer a save feature. We recommend taking screenshots or noting down your inputs and results for future reference.

How often should I update my calculations?

+

It's a good idea to revisit your calculations annually or whenever there's a significant change in your financial situation or goals.

How does inflation affect these projections?

+

Our calculator doesn't directly account for inflation. To factor in inflation, you can adjust your expected return rate to be a "real" return rate (nominal return minus expected inflation rate).

Can I input multiple income sources or investments?

+

Currently, the calculator is designed for a single investment stream. For multiple income sources, you might need to combine them into a single input or run separate calculations.
