Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the projections?

Our calculator uses the information you provide to make projections. While we strive for accuracy, actual results may vary due to market fluctuations and other factors.

Can I save my calculations?

Yes, you can save your calculations by downloading them as a CSV file. After performing a calculation, you'll see a download button at the top right of the results table. Clicking this button will allow you to save a CSV file containing all the calculated data. This file can be opened in spreadsheet software for further analysis or record-keeping. Additionally, you can always take screenshots or note down your inputs for future reference.

How often should I update my calculations?

It's a good idea to revisit your calculations annually or whenever there's a significant change in your financial situation or goals.

How does inflation affect these projections?

Our calculator doesn't directly account for inflation. To factor in inflation, you can adjust your expected return rate to be a "real" return rate (nominal return minus expected inflation rate).

Can I input multiple income sources or investments?

Currently, the calculator is designed for a single investment stream. For multiple income sources, you might need to combine them into a single input or run separate calculations.

How was this calculator created?

This Financial Future Planner, including its calculator and FAQ, was generated using AI within a 30-minute timeframe. It demonstrates the potential of AI in rapidly creating functional and informative web tools.

Is financial advice provided with this tool?

No, this tool is for educational and illustrative purposes only. It does not provide personalized financial advice. For specific financial guidance, please consult with a qualified financial advisor.

How can AI be used in financial planning?

AI can be used in various aspects of financial planning, including risk assessment, portfolio optimization, fraud detection, and creating personalized financial plans. However, human oversight and expertise remain crucial in financial decision-making.

Key Features

  • Client-side calculations for speed and privacy
  • Automatic detection of user's preferred number format and currency
